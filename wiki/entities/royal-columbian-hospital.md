# Royal Columbian Hospital (RCH)
**Type**: Organization
**First seen**: sapperton-master-strategy-2026, 2026-07-07
**Last confirmed**: sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026 (Jul 2026), 2026-07-07

## What we know
Confirmed by both 2026 sources (strengthened):
- BC's only SkyTrain-adjacent hospital (Sapperton Station, Expo Line); Level 1 trauma + cardiac + neurosurgery + high-risk obstetrics + NICU + acute mental health on one campus
- 607,859 sq ft campus across E Columbia/Sherbrooke/Allen/Keary/Brunette; UBC Faculty of Medicine affiliate
- $1.5B three-phase redevelopment; **Jim Pattison Acute Care Tower opened May 31, 2026**: 388 beds (+~50% capacity), 47-bed ICU, 48-bed obstetrics + NICU, 75-space ED, 17 ORs; $30M Jim Pattison donation [memo cites Fraser Health release May 2026; Province release 2026INF0030-000583]
- Workforce: 3,300+ employees (2017 baseline); 600–612+ open postings Jun 2026
- New Westminster OCP designates a Special Employment Area within 5-min walk of campus [sapperton-master-strategy-2026]
- Described as city's largest employer [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026]
