# Transit-Oriented Area (TOA) Framework — New Westminster / BC
**Type**: Policy
**First seen**: sapperton-master-strategy-2026, 2026-07-07
**Last confirmed**: sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026 (Jul 2026), 2026-07-07

## What we know
- BC provincial TOA legislation compels density near SkyTrain; New Westminster implemented via **TOA Designation Bylaw No. 8460, adopted June 2024** [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026, §2]
- Tier structure by station distance: Tier 3 (400–800m) → 8 storeys / 3.00 FSR minimum; Tier 2 → 12 storeys eligible (Keary/Sherbrooke blocks) [both 2026 sources]
- Approval path for TOA mid-rise on RS-1 land: full rezoning + public hearing; **design guidelines expected 2027** [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026, §2]
- Council's stated preference in TOA rezonings: 100% secured purpose-built rental [both 2026 sources]
- Sapperton TOA land pricing: ~$430–$530/sq ft (sponsor estimate); comp: Cedar Court + Griffiths Manor, 41,382 sq ft listed $21M / $507 per sq ft (Goodman Commercial, Oct 2024) [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026, §4]
