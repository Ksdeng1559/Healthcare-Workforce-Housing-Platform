# Healthcare Workforce Housing Platform
**Type**: Project (product/platform — this repository)
**First seen**: platform-repo-docs, 2026-07-07
**Last confirmed**: platform-repo-docs, 2026-07-07

## What we know
- The umbrella product: "a planning, research, and coordination system for creating sustainable rental housing near major hospitals" — owner Eng & Associates, PRD v1.0 Draft [platform-repo-docs]
- Seven pillars: healthcare demand, transit-oriented land assembly, purpose-built rental, public financing readiness, green rental strategy, modular feasibility, partner coordination
- MVP: GitHub-based knowledge base + planning repository (this repo; this wiki implements much of it)
- Modules planned: hospital demand, land assembly tracker, program readiness, green rental, modular delivery, partner tracker, reporting
- [[sapperton-district-project]] is demonstration case #1 — "first repeatable playbook for hospital-adjacent workforce rental housing"
- Success metrics: hospital markets evaluated, priority sites, parcels tracked, partner conversations, briefs generated
