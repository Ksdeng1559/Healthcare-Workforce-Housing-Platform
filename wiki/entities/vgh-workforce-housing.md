# VGH Workforce Housing (Harrison Street + PC Urban)
**Type**: Project (external precedent)
**First seen**: sapperton-master-strategy-2026, 2026-07-07
**Last confirmed**: sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026 (Jul 2026), 2026-07-07

## What we know
Confirmed by both 2026 sources (which cite Harrison Street / PC Urban press releases Mar 2026, RENX, Connect CRE):
- Announced Mar 31, 2026 — described as Canada's first purpose-built hospital workforce housing development
- Twin 22-storey towers, W 13th Ave & Willow, across from Vancouver General Hospital
- C$265M; 507 units (78 studio / 351 one-bed / 78 two-bed); fully furnished, utilities + Wi-Fi included; shift-based leasing; 11,000 sq ft childcare
- Nomination agreement gives VGH employees priority access below comparable new-product rents
- Groundbreaking target Aug 2026; delivery Jun 2029
- Quote: "There wasn't an existing policy framework for this kind of housing… this could serve as a precedent for future workforce housing" — Brent Sawchyn, CEO, PC Urban

## Role in wiki domain
