**Type**: Organization (federal housing agency)
**First seen**: sapperton-master-strategy-2026, 2026-07-07
**Last confirmed**: modular-housing-brief-2026 (Jul 2026), 2026-07-07

## What we know
- Launched September 14, 2025 with $13B initial capitalization; Build Canada Homes Act (permanent Crown corporation) introduced Feb 5, 2026 [modular-housing-brief-2026]
- Administers the [[affordable-housing-fund]] (AHF folded into BCH mandate) [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026]
- Prefab-specific capital: $1B equity + $25B debt for Canadian prefabricated builders (separate from the $13B) [modular-housing-brief-2026]
- Direct Build: bulk factory-built orders across 6 federal sites (Toronto, Winnipeg, Edmonton, Ottawa, Dartmouth, Longueuil), 4,000 prefab homes, construction from 2026 [modular-housing-brief-2026]
- AHF $1.5B top-up announced Sep 2025 [sapperton-master-strategy-2026]
