## What we know
Proposed multifamily apartment building on the [[garrett-street-assembly]] site. All figures below are proponent estimates [garrett-street-proposal-2024].

**Two development scenarios:**
| Scenario | FAR | Buildable | Units | Height |
|----------|-----|-----------|-------|--------|
| RM-3 | 2.5 | 82,500 sq ft | 90 | ≤6 storeys |
| TOD Guidelines | 3.0 | 99,000 sq ft | 100+ | 6–8 storeys |

**Financial estimates (Dec 2024, unverified):**
- Sale price: $700K–$800K/unit → gross revenue $63M–$80M
- Construction: $550/sq ft; soft costs 15–20% of total
- Land cost: TBD — no full pro forma exists in sources

**Timeline estimate:** 3–4 years total (rezoning 8–12 mo, permits 6–8 mo, construction 18–24 mo)

**Committed/offered features:** underground parking, LEED-or-equivalent, green roofs, EV charging, bike storage, rooftop terrace, mixed unit sizes (1–3 BR), possible ground-floor retail or childcare

**Public benefits (offered, not committed):** potential affordable units via density bonus; DCC/CAC contributions
