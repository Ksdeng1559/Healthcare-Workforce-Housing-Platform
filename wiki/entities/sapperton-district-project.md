# Sapperton Healthcare Housing District
**Type**: Project
**First seen**: sapperton-master-strategy-2026 (Jun 2026), 2026-07-07
**Last confirmed**: sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026 (Jul 2026), 2026-07-07

## What we know
The platform's core initiative: a phased, employer-anchored purpose-built rental district in the Garrett Street / Sapperton corridor serving [[royal-columbian-hospital]] workforce. Sponsored by [[eng-associates]]. Pre-feasibility stage; no capital committed as of Jul 2026 [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026, §5].

**Three phases** [sapperton-master-strategy-2026]:
| Phase | Years | Scope | Units |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 — Proof of Concept | 2026–2028 | Acquire [[garrett-407-419-assembly]]; 6-unit infill DP (no rezoning); healthcare-worker rental; CMHC ACLP + MLI Select | 6–12 |
| 2 — Corridor Assembly | 2027–2030 | +3–5 lots → 15,000–25,000 sq ft; TOA mid-rise rezoning; 8 storeys / 3.0 FSR; 100% secured rental | 80–150 |
| 3 — District Vision | 2029–2034 | 30–60-lot land study; bespoke city district designation; 3–5 buildings; mixed tenure; clinic/childcare ground floors | 300–500+ |

**Phase 1 economics** (estimates): TPC $3,764,375; rents $2,600–$3,200/mo; NOI ~$140K–$173K; cap 4.5–5.0%; stabilized ~$2.8M–$3.8M [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026, §4]

**Phase 2 capital stack** (illustrative): ACLP 100% LTC ($18M–$22M) → MLI Select (95% LTV, 50-yr am); AHF forgivable $2M–$5M; Fraser Health master lease 30–50 units (~$800K–$1.5M/yr, 10-yr); sponsor equity + institutional co-invest 15–20% [sapperton-master-strategy-2026]

**Target partners** (none committed): QuadReal/BCI, Wesgroup, Harrison Street AM, HOOPP (high fit); BC Housing, OMERS/Oxford (moderate) [sapperton-investment-memorandum-2026, §5]

**Policy hooks**: OCP Special Employment Area around RCH; NW 8,137-unit housing target by 2029; HAF deadline Jun 15, 2026; council TOA preference for 100% secured rental; provincial healthcare staffing shortage [sapperton-master-strategy-2026]
