# Green Rental Housing Strategy

## Purpose

The Healthcare Workforce Housing Platform should position sustainability as a core part of the investment and development thesis.

Green rental housing can support lower operating costs, stronger long-term asset performance, improved tenant comfort, and better alignment with public-sector housing and climate objectives.

## Strategy Components

- Energy-efficient building design
- High-performance building envelope
- Efficient mechanical systems
- Reduced operating costs
- Emissions reduction
- Transit-oriented location
- Walkable healthcare community
- Sustainability reporting
- Green financing readiness

## Application to Sapperton

The Sapperton Healthcare Housing District should be positioned as a green healthcare workforce housing district anchored by Royal Columbian Hospital and Sapperton transit access.
