# Executive Summary

## Healthcare Workforce Housing Platform

The Healthcare Workforce Housing Platform is designed to organize and scale the creation of sustainable rental housing near major hospitals.

The first demonstration project is the Sapperton Healthcare Housing District near Royal Columbian Hospital in New Westminster, British Columbia.

## Opportunity

Hospitals are expanding, but nearby housing supply often does not meet the needs of healthcare workers. This creates commute burden, recruitment pressure, retention challenges, and staff wellness concerns.

The platform treats healthcare workforce housing as essential community infrastructure.

## Platform Pillars

1. Healthcare workforce demand
2. Transit-oriented land assembly
3. Purpose-built rental housing
4. Public financing readiness
5. Green rental housing strategy
6. Modular construction feasibility
7. Institutional partner coordination

## Initial Focus

The Sapperton use case will establish the first repeatable playbook for hospital-adjacent workforce rental housing.
