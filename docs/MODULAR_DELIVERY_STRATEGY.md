# Modular Delivery Strategy

## Purpose

The platform should evaluate modular and prefabricated construction as a potential delivery method for healthcare workforce housing.

## Benefits to Evaluate

- Faster delivery
- Reduced construction disruption
- Better quality control
- Reduced weather delay risk
- Potential operating and cost efficiencies
- Repeatable building systems

## Key Risks

- Factory capacity
- Transportation logistics
- Site access
- Local design requirements
- Factory-stage financing
- Contract structuring

## Application to Sapperton

Modular delivery should be evaluated as a preferred option where it improves timing, financing, cost certainty, and sustainability outcomes. It should not be treated as mandatory until site and supplier feasibility are confirmed.
