# Product Requirements Document

## Healthcare Workforce Housing Platform

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
Owner: Eng & Associates

## Executive Summary

The Healthcare Workforce Housing Platform is a planning, research, and coordination system for creating sustainable rental housing near major hospitals.

The first use case is the Sapperton Healthcare Housing District near Royal Columbian Hospital in New Westminster, British Columbia.

The opportunity is based on a clear market problem: healthcare systems are expanding, but many healthcare workers struggle to find suitable housing near the hospitals where they work. This can increase commute time, reduce staff wellness, and make recruitment and retention more difficult for healthcare employers.

## Product Vision

Create a repeatable operating platform that helps identify, plan, finance, and communicate healthcare workforce housing opportunities.

The platform should support hospital demand research, land assembly planning, zoning review, public financing readiness, green rental housing strategy, modular construction feasibility, partner coordination, and investor-grade reporting.

## Core Modules

### Hospital Demand Module

Tracks hospital size, workforce demand, expansion status, transit access, and nearby housing need.

### Land Assembly Module

Tracks parcels, zoning, lot size, frontage, ownership status, outreach status, and assembly feasibility.

### Program Readiness Module

Tracks readiness for rental housing programs, affordability requirements, accessibility goals, energy targets, and required documents.

### Green Rental Housing Module

Tracks energy efficiency, operating cost reduction, emissions reduction, and sustainability reporting.

### Modular Delivery Module

Assesses whether modular or prefabricated construction is suitable for a project.

### Partner Tracker

Tracks partner type, role, fit score, contact status, meetings, and next actions.

### Reporting Module

Generates executive summaries, project briefs, scorecards, risk registers, and strategy memos.

## Initial Case Study: Sapperton Healthcare Housing District

The Sapperton Healthcare Housing District is the first demonstration use case for the platform.

The project is anchored by Royal Columbian Hospital and the surrounding Sapperton transit-oriented area. The objective is to create a repeatable model for healthcare workforce rental housing that can begin with a small proof-of-concept and grow into a broader district strategy.
